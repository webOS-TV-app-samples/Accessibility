webOS TV Accessibility Settings

How to Change Accessibility Settings on TV

  1. Press ⚙ button on your remote.
  2. Enter into General > Accessibility.
  3. Select an item that you want to change (Audio Guidance, High Contrast, Closed Caption).
  4. Press OK button to toggle the setting.
